<p>You can create a subsystem in two ways:</p>
<ul>
<li>Add a subsystem block to your model, then open that block and add the blocks it contains to the subsystem window.</li>
<li>Add the blocks that make up the subsystem, then group those blocks into a subsystem.</li>
